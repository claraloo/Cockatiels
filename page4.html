Cockatiel Types

Scroll down for the answers!

Funfact: The 3 birds in the first picture hatched within a few days of each other!
The left most bird is probably a pied cockatiel.
The middle bird is seen with a pearl pattern here but after moulting, its patterns changed to look more like a normal grey.
The rightmost bird is likely a cinnamon pied cockatiel due to its slightly fainter grey feathers.

The bird in the 2nd picture is likely to be a pied cockatiel as it has light yellow feathers on the body and some variation of grey/white feathers on the back

The last bird in the third picture is likely to be a white-faced pearl as it has only white and grey feathers and small pearl like patterns
